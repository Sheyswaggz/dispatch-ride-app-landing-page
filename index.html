How It Works

Getting a ride with Dispatch is simple and straightforward. Follow these easy steps to get moving.

  1. Download the App

    Get the Dispatch app from the App Store or Google Play. Sign up with your email or phone number in seconds.

  2. Request Your Ride

    Enter your destination and choose your ride type. See the fare estimate and request your ride with one tap.

  3. Track Your Driver

    Watch your driver arrive in real-time on the map. Get notified when they're nearby and see their vehicle details.

  4. Enjoy Your Ride

    Sit back and relax as your driver takes you to your destination. Pay automatically and rate your experience.
